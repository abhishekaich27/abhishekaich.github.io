    About me:

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the Video Computing Group of University of California, Riverside under the supervision of Dr. Amit Roy-Chowdhury since Fall '18.

Prior to joining UCR, I obtained my M.S. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology (NIT), Tiruchirappalli (India) in 2018 supervised by Dr. P. Palanisamy. I obtained my B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT), Odisha (India) in 2015.
